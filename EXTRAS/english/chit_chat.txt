Daily conversation, examples:

- How are you? 
	- not too bad, and you?							- no mal, y tu?
	- nothing much (very informal)						- nada interesante
	- good, and yourself?							- todo bien, y tu?
	- Im wrecked, lot of work						- estoy muerto de cansancio
	- Fine, fine, and you?							- bien, bien, y tu?

- How was your day?								
	- actually quite busy							- realmente bien ocupado
	- quite relaxing							- realmente tranquilo (relajante)
	- I didnt have many classes today					- No tuve muchas clases hoy
	- this is my last class today						- esta clase es ultima
	- very exhausting							- muy cansativo
	
- How was your weekend?
	- I went to see my parents (In... Bogota)				- fui visitar mis papas
	- nothing much, I stayed home 						- nada interesante, me quede en la casa
	- on saturday I went with friends for a party				- sabado me fui con amigos para fiesta
	- I went to the cinema							- yo fui para cine
	- I was studying the whole weekend (lot of homeworks)			- estaba estudiando todo el fin de semana
